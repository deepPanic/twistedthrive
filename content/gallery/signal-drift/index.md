Experience is a virtue. I decontextualize, and then I reconstruct. My photography looks past the obvious, relying on close observation and engagement with my subjects. Viewing my work puts you inside a moment, allowing you to experience the laughter, sadness, confusion, and bliss shared in a single expression. I aim to elevate the overlooked, inviting the audience to see their ordinary lives in a completely new light.
